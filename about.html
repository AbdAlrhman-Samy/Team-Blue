Other responsibilities:  Attend Executive Committee meetings and assist the committees.  Ensure that information (via email generally) from IEEE Headquarters is transmitted to the student Officers.  Participate in Regional Activities Committee meetings.  Consult with Egypt Section SAC Chair, Regional SAC Chair, or Region Eight Director about activities or problems.

Head of Media

The Media Committee is in charge of the team's brand and advertising it, to show it to the public in a specific form. The committee in IEEE BUB SB is divided into 4 sections: - Designers They are the ones who design all the advertisements, whether online like the posts that you see on the page or offline, like banners, flyers, IDs... etc. - Content Creators They are responsible for the content posted on Social Media and YouTube channel. - Photographers They are responsible for filming and documenting the events. - Video Editing They are the ones responsible for making and editing all videos that can be posted on Facebook page and YouTube channel.
